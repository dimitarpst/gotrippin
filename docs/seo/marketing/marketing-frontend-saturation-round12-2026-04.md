## 2. Deep dives (abbrev)

### 2.1 Hostelworld — social layer on a transaction site

- **Differentiator:** **Hostel Chat / City Chat / Linkups** — “See who’s going” before trip; reviews emphasize **solo female** safety of knowing who’s around.
- **Deals:** %-off tiles with **review counts** — **C** + **E** in one card.
- **i18n:** Long **language** footer list — hreflang / locale **surface** (contrast gotrippin single-URL Phase 1).
- **Takeaway:** **Social** proof for group trips is adjacent; we avoid **stranger-DM** scope until product + safety model exist.

### 2.2 Viator — TripAdvisor sibling, fetch variance

- **H1:** “Do more with Viator” + **scale number** (300k+).
- **Blocks:** Why book, top destinations, **attraction** grids with **tour counts**, warm destinations, free cancellation.
- **Takeaway:** **Round 11 timeout** vs **Round 12 OK** — same lesson as Expedia/TA: **time/network** sensitivity; always label **session** when comparing rounds.

### 2.3 OnTheSnow — conditions as daily SEO

- **Core:** **Featured cams**, **snowfall** leaderboard, **first-hand** blurbs (timestamped) — **UGC** flywheel.
- **Editorial:** Pass guides (Epic, Indy), “where to ski this month” — **D** for skiers, not planners.
- **Takeaway:** **Vertical** freshness beats generic trip landing; gotrippin only copies **“live/recent”** patterns if we have **real** data (weather, etc.).

### 2.4 REI × Intrepid — partnership FAQ as product spec

- **Structure:** **Exclusive** vs **Recommended** collections; **member** pricing; **1%** to Cooperative Action Fund; **phone/email** redemption for promos.
- **FAQ:** Relationship, geography, growth — answers read like **internal PRD**.
- **Legal:** **Seller of travel** disclosure — Intrepid merchant of record; REI marketing partner.
- **Takeaway:** Any **co-branded** trip supply on gotrippin needs **equally explicit** who charges, who supports, who is liable.

### 2.5 Lonely Planet — media brand selling **Journeys**

- **Tiles:** Books / Stories / **Journeys**; priced **multi-day** packages (Norway cruise, Japan Golden Route, …).
- **Partners:** Insurance, eSIM — **affiliate** row.
- **Takeaway:** **Editorial trust → transaction** is a second funnel; we stay **software-first** unless **Journeys-like** tours are real.
